About Me

I'm from Flensburg, studied media informatics and currently studying applied computer science. I'm curious how things are built – and prefer figuring out how they work. Design matters to me, both the kind you see and the kind you don't.

I spend time with tabletop RPGs, mostly »Das schwarze Auge«, building things with microcontrollers, watching corvids and parrots solve problems, playing story-rich games and appreciating good sushi with friends. I value good engineering regardless of where it shows up. I think sleep or showers solve more problems than most people give them credit for.

Things I think about

  • Good design is good design, regardless of the domain
  • Most complex things are just simple things composed badly
  • The best way to understand something is trying to rebuild it yourself
  • Nothing is so urgent that you can't take time to think it through
  • Ravens solving problems and people debugging code follow surprisingly similar patterns
  • The best debugging tools are still a good night's sleep, a shower and a duck
